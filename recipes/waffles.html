Waffles

Description

This recipe comes to life through a flavorful combination of cheeses, chiles, and tortillas.

This recipe is ideal when you need to cook for a large group of people in a pinch

It can also make good use of leftovers

Ingredients

  • chiles poblanos
  • chiles de arbol, secos y tostados
  • chile ancho
  • tortillas
  • cebolla

Steps

  1. Place pan with water on heat until it begins to boil
  2. Once boiling remove water from heat and add in the ancho, california, and the chiles de arbol
  3. After most wrinkles are gone from peppers, place them in the blender (3 minutes)
  4. Add two pieces of garlic, two pepper corn balls, one clove bud, and a tablespoon of chicken boullion
  5. Blend it all until sauce is smooth.
  6. drain sauce onto pan
  7. Add 1 tbsp of oil into the mix and stir until a soft boil begins.
  8. After it softly boils for 3 minutes, you can remove the pan from the heat.
  9. Turn on another pan and add enough oil to submerge a white tortilla.
  10. Add a tortilla into the pan with oil until it changes to a darker yellow. Once you see it bubbling you can turn down the heat a little bit.
  11. Coat tortillas in sauce you made and lay them out on a sheet.
  12. Stuff tortillas with your choice of chicken, potatos, other meats and even seafood.
  13. After tortillas have been stuffed, bake at 350m for 10minutes.
  14. Enjoy!
